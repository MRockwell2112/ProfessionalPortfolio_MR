Welcome to my 3D & Animation section. Below you can check out my blender models with photoshop editing included!

This is my youtube channel where I animate and show timelapses of models/graphics. I started out with SourceFilmmaker to get started learning animation and it's fundamentals.

As you can see, a lot of my content is Team Fortress 2 related, and now that I'm in Blender I can create my own characters, which was a limitation of SFM. My microphone model was my most recent model, as I already learned emission and raytracing from my PC model, I decided to learn animation and keyframing in Blenders interface using the changing colors of my microphone.

A note I'll add. The black perspective bars I have in my graphics is actually a custom rig I made in the Blender scripting tab, since I know Python, I can create custom scripts to make my life easier!

This is the script I wrote to spawn the rig. It has a root bone, a scale transform, etc. Both borders have their own armature but are linked to the root for animation purposes!
